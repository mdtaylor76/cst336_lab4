Welcome to the Mercury Page!

Mercury is the first and closes planet to the sun in our solar system. Mercury orbits the sun once every 88 days, which is the shortest in the solar system. Mercury used to be considered the seconds smallest planet, but with Pluto’s demotion, Mercury is now the smallest planet in the solar system. Mercury has little ability to retain heat due to its lack of atmosphere. So, despite being closer to the sun than any other planet, Mercury is not the hottest planet in the solar system. It does, however, features the solar system’s largest difference in temperatures, ranging from -280 F to 800 F.
